## Privacy Notes

- Keep unrelated private work in separate repositories.
- Invite collaborators only to repositories they need.
- Do not store sensitive personal, client, medical, financial, or legal material unless the repository privacy settings and user consent are appropriate.
- For genealogy projects, take special care with living people.
